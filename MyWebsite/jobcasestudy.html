JobTrack

This case study explores the design and development of a job application tracking system that helps job seekers manage their applications, interviews, and follow-ups in one centralized platform.

Objective

Create an intuitive platform that helps job seekers track applications, manage deadlines, and prepare for interviews while reducing stress and improving organization.

Target Audience

Job seekers at all levels, from recent graduates to experienced professionals changing careers or looking for new opportunities.

Results

Beta testers reported a 35% reduction in missed follow-ups and a 50% improvement in interview preparation confidence.

View Full Case Study

Design Process

Our user-centered approach focused on reducing job search anxiety through organization and proactive features.

Research

Interviewed 30+ job seekers to identify pain points in the application process and common organizational challenges.

Wireframing

Created low-fidelity prototypes focusing on quick data entry and at-a-glance status information.

UI Design

Developed a clean, professional interface with visual indicators for application status and priority.

Testing

Conducted usability tests with real users to refine the workflow and ensure intuitive navigation.

Key Features

Strategic design decisions that enhanced user experience and reduced job search stress.

Dashboard Overview

Visual summary of application status, upcoming interviews, and pending follow-ups at a glance.

Reminder System

Automated reminders for follow-ups, thank-you notes, and interview preparation tasks.

Progress Analytics

Track application response rates, interview conversion, and identify areas for improvement.

Resume & Cover Letter Tracking

Version control for application documents tailored to specific job postings.

Development Timeline

The iterative process of designing and refining JobTrack Pro

Research Phase

Conducted user interviews and competitive analysis to identify key user needs and market gaps.

Wireframing

UI Design

Developed high-fidelity mockups with a focus on accessibility and mobile responsiveness.

Beta Testing
