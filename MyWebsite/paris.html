Paris, France

Capital of France
5.0 (2,417 reviews)
The most romantic city in the world

Overview

Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most iconic destinations in the world. Known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture, Paris offers an unforgettable experience with its beautiful boulevards, world-class museums, and romantic atmosphere.

From the majestic Eiffel Tower to the artistic treasures of the Louvre and the bohemian charm of Montmartre, Paris captivates visitors with its timeless beauty. The city's café culture, haute cuisine, and architectural marvels make it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking both sophistication and romance.

Best Time to Visit

April-June & September-October (Mild weather)

Language

French (English widely spoken in tourist areas)

Currency

Euro (€)

Top Highlights

Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris offers breathtaking views from its observation decks and a sparkling light show at night.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of other masterpieces, the world's largest art museum.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture with stunning stained glass and gargoyles.

French Cuisine

From croissants to coq au vin, experience world-renowned French gastronomy in charming bistros.
